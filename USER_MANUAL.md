# 📖 User Manual — Tax Transparency Tracker

This guide explains how to use the Tax Transparency Tracker as a citizen.

---

## 🖥️ Accessing the App

Open the app in your browser at the deployed URL (or `http://localhost:3000` if running locally).

No account or login required — it's fully open to all citizens.

---

## 🔢 Step 1: Enter Your Income

On the home screen, you'll see the **Tax Calculator** panel.

1. Enter your **annual income** in the input field (e.g. ₹8,00,000)
2. Select your **tax regime** — New Regime (2024–25) or Old Regime
3. Click **Calculate**

The app will show:
- Your estimated total tax paid
- Your effective tax rate
- How much you contribute per day

---

## 📊 Step 2: See Your Sector Breakdown

After calculating, scroll down to see the **Allocation Breakdown**.

Your tax is split across 8 sectors:

| Sector | What it funds |
|---|---|
| Infrastructure | Roads, highways, bridges, metro |
| Defence | Armed forces, border security |
| Healthcare | Govt hospitals, Ayushman Bharat |
| Education | Schools, IITs, scholarships |
| Social Welfare | PM Kisan, MGNREGA |
| Rural Development | Villages, sanitation, water |
| Administration | Govt salaries, courts |
| Others | Space, environment, sports |

Each sector shows the **exact rupee amount** from your tax and a progress bar showing its share.

---

## 🗺️ Step 3: Explore the Project Map

Click the **Map** tab to see government-funded projects near you.

- 📍 Each pin is a real funded project (roads, hospitals, schemes)
- Click any pin to see the project name, budget allocated, and current status
- Filter by sector using the dropdown on the left

---

## 🤖 Step 4: Ask the AI Chatbot

Click the **Ask AI** button (bottom right) to open the chatbot.

You can ask questions like:
- *"Where did my ₹42,000 go?"*
- *"How much was spent on education in Telangana?"*
- *"Is the Hyderabad Metro Phase 2 on track?"*
- *"What is Jal Jeevan Mission?"*

The chatbot supports **Hindi, Telugu, and English**. Just type naturally.

---

## 🚩 Step 5: View Anomaly Flags

The **Alerts** tab shows AI-flagged irregularities in public spending:

- 🔴 **High risk** — suspected overbilling or stalled project
- 🟡 **Medium risk** — budget deviation above 20%
- 🟢 **Normal** — spending within expected range

These are generated by our ML model and are meant for awareness and further investigation.

---

## ❓ FAQ

**Is my income data stored anywhere?**
No. All calculations happen in your browser. We do not store any personal data.

**How often is the data updated?**
Budget data is updated annually after the Union Budget presentation. Project data is updated monthly from data.gov.in.

**Can I share my breakdown?**
Yes — click the **Share** button on your breakdown screen to copy a shareable link.
